# Vision

> **Starter template.** `/bootstrap` fills this in from your answers. Replace the
> example text with the answers for your project — keep the headings.

## What is this project?

One or two sentences. *Example: "A web app that lets neighbours share grocery lists and
split the costs."*

## Why does it exist?

The problem it solves, or the need it addresses. *Example: "Couriers and small flat
shares end up double-buying the same staples; tracking who paid for what is fiddly."*

## Who is it for?

Target users and their context. *Example: "Households of 2–6 adults sharing one
kitchen, using phones."*

## What does success look like?

The outcome that means this project worked. *Example: "Users open the app weekly, and
say monthly settlement takes under 2 minutes per person."*

## Non-goals

What this project deliberately does NOT do. *Example: "Not an inventory tracker; not a
recipe app; not a payments processor — settlement is informal."*
